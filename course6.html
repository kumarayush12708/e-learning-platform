Business and Entrepreneurship

Course Overview

This course explores the foundational principles of business and entrepreneurship. Topics include business planning, marketing, and financial management. By the end of the course, you will have a solid understanding of key concepts for starting and running a business.

Course Syllabus

  • Module 1: Introduction to Business Principles
  • Module 2: Marketing Strategies
  • Module 3: Financial Management Basics
  • Module 4: Business Planning and Entrepreneurship

Course Materials

Below are the recommended materials for this course:

  • Textbook: "Business Essentials" by Business Expert
  • Online Resources: Links to relevant websites, business tools, and case studies
