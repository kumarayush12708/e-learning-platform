Music Theory

Course Overview

This course explores the fundamental principles of music theory. Topics include notation, scales, chords, and basic composition. By the end of the course, you will have a solid understanding of the theoretical aspects of music.

Course Syllabus

  • Module 1: Introduction to Music Notation
  • Module 2: Understanding Scales and Chords
  • Module 3: Basic Music Composition
  • Module 4: Music Theory in Practice

Course Materials

Below are the recommended materials for this course:

  • Textbook: "Music Theory Essentials" by Music Expert
  • Online Resources: Links to sheet music, music theory tutorials, and composition tools
