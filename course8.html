Mathematics for Everyone

Course Overview

This course aims to make mathematics accessible to everyone. Topics include basic arithmetic, algebra, geometry, and problem-solving techniques. By the end of the course, you will have a solid foundation in fundamental mathematical concepts.

Course Syllabus

  • Module 1: Basic Arithmetic
  • Module 2: Algebraic Concepts
  • Module 3: Geometry Fundamentals
  • Module 4: Problem-Solving Techniques

Course Materials

Below are the recommended materials for this course:

  • Textbook: "Mathematics Made Easy" by Math Expert
  • Online Resources: Links to relevant websites, math tutorials, and practice problems
