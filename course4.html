Graphic Design Essentials

Course Overview

This course covers the essential principles of graphic design. Topics include color theory, typography, and layout design. By the end of the course, you will be equipped with the fundamental skills needed for graphic design projects.

Course Syllabus

  • Module 1: Introduction to Graphic Design
  • Module 2: Color Theory and Application
  • Module 3: Typography in Design
  • Module 4: Layout Design Principles

Course Materials

Below are the recommended materials for this course:

  • Textbook: "Graphic Design Fundamentals" by Design Expert
  • Online Resources: Links to relevant websites, design tools, and tutorials
