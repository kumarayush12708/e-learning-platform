Overview

if patients suffering from ailments receive the right type of blood, their lives can be saved. So when you are writing a report about a blood donation camp, you need to mention where the camp took place, who were present in the camp and whether doctors or any medical professionals were present to oversee it or not.

Whole blood donation

The blood is provided by the blood bank to the people who are in need. Blood banks obtain blood from the healthy volunteers and supply it to the patients in need of it. If the blood that has been collected is not to be used immediately, it is stored in blood banks (under the specific required conditions) for some days.
