Art History

Course Overview

This course delves into the history of art and its various movements. Topics include key art periods, famous artists, and art appreciation. By the end of the course, you will have a comprehensive understanding of the evolution of art through time.

Course Syllabus

  • Module 1: Ancient Art and Classical Periods
  • Module 2: Renaissance and Baroque Art
  • Module 3: Romanticism to Modern Art
  • Module 4: Contemporary Art Movements

Course Materials

Below are the recommended materials for this course:

  • Textbook: "Art Through the Ages" by Art Historian
  • Online Resources: Links to art history databases, museum collections, and documentaries
